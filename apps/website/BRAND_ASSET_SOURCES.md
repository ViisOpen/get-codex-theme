# Brand asset sources

Get Codex Theme uses these marks only to identify the subject of an independent,
unofficial fan concept. The marks render inside a code-built workspace preview,
are not baked into generated backgrounds, and are not included in downloadable
theme packs. Get Codex Theme is not sponsored, approved, or affiliated with any
company listed below.

## Simple Icons marks

Meta, Apple, Tesla, and Anthropic marks are rendered from the
`simple-icons` npm package, version 16.26.0. Simple Icons publishes its icon
artwork under [CC0 1.0](https://github.com/simple-icons/simple-icons/blob/develop/LICENSE.md)
and documents brand-specific usage guidance in its icon metadata. Trademark
rights remain with the respective owners; the CC0 license does not grant a
trademark license.

Company brand guidelines can change. Review the relevant official guidance
before moving any mark beyond this clearly labeled editorial preview context.
