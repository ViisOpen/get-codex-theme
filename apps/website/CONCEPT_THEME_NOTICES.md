# Concept theme artwork and status

The artwork under `public/concept-themes/` was created specifically for Get
Codex Theme with OpenAI's built-in image generation workflow. The prompts
require landscape composition, a low-detail interface-safe area, no text, no
logos, no watermark, and no copied character or product design.

These files are **concept preview artwork**, not downloadable theme packs and
not verified screenshots from Codex Desktop. A concept becomes a downloadable
pack only after responsive crops, tokens, a local installer, a restore path,
and current-version compatibility checks are complete.

The concept preview artwork is not included in the repository's MIT software
license or the CC BY 4.0 license used by the separately published free packs.
All rights are reserved unless a future per-pack `LICENSE-ASSETS.txt` says
otherwise.

Pages that use company names are independent, unofficial inspiration studies.
Their background artwork does not contain a generated logo, product screenshot,
branded hardware, or copied company artwork. A code-rendered workspace preview
integrates an unmodified brand-identifying mark so the subject is clear,
alongside a visible `Unofficial fan concept` label and non-affiliation notice.
Those marks are not included in downloadable theme packs. All trademarks belong
to their respective owners. Get Codex Theme is not sponsored, approved, or
affiliated with those companies. See `BRAND_ASSET_SOURCES.md` for provenance.

No gallery art, character art, branded theme assets, or composite previews from
`tree0519/Codex-Dream-Skin-Forge` or `Fei-Away/Codex-Dream-Skin` were copied or
adapted for this catalog.
